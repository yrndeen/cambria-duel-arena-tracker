How It Works

Our platform connects directly to the Abstract L2 blockchain to fetch real-time data from the Cambria Duel Arena smart contracts. We process and analyze this data to provide:

  • Individual wallet performance tracking
  • Duel history and outcome analysis
  • Profit and loss calculations
  • Win rate statistics
  • Volume and activity metrics
  • Comparative performance analysis

All data is sourced directly from on-chain transactions, ensuring accuracy and transparency in our analytics.

How Duels Work

The Cambria Duel Arena follows a specific flow for each duel:

  1. Duel Initiation (initBattle): Player 1 creates a duel challenge with a specific wager amount and opponent address
  2. Duel Status - Pending: The duel waits for Player 2 to accept the challenge
  3. Duel Joining (joinBattle): Player 2 accepts the duel by calling joinBattle with the same wager amount
  4. Duel Status - Active: Both players have committed their wagers and the duel is in progress
  5. Duel Completion (claimProceeds): The winner claims both wagers minus the platform fee
  6. Duel Status - Completed: The duel is finished and funds have been distributed
  7. Duel Cancellation (nullifyBattle): Either player can cancel a pending duel to get their wager refunded
  8. Duel Status - Cancelled: The duel was cancelled and players received refunds

Our platform tracks all these events in real-time, showing you the current status of each duel and providing detailed transaction history.

Duel Arena Fee Structure

The Cambria Duel Arena operates on a simple but important fee structure that affects all profit calculations:

  • Equal Wagers: Both players must wager the same amount of ETH
  • Winner Takes All: The winner receives both wagers (2x the duel amount)
  • 5% Platform Fee: Winners pay a 5% fee on the total winnings
  • Net Profit Calculation: Winner's net profit = (2 √ó wager) - (0.05 √ó 2 √ó wager) = 1.9 √ó wager

Example: In a 0.01 ETH duel, the winner claims 0.02 ETH but pays 0.001 ETH in fees, resulting in a net profit of 0.019 ETH.

All profit calculations on this platform reflect net profits after the 5% fee deduction, giving you the most accurate picture of actual earnings.
